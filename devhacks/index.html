DevHacks #1

A little bit goes a long way

DevHacks is an open online hackathon organized by DevChat and sponsored by DETA. Join now!

Sign Up with a Team Name and your email. After signing up, you will receive a team ID.
To form a team, get a partner to provide it in the "Team Name" field when they sign up.

For whom?

The hackathon is open for everyone around the globe and aims to help developers code together, especially beginners. To qualify for the prizes, you need to join DevChat.

Only solo developers and teams with up to 2 members can participate.

Team matching

If you don't have a partner, but would like to hack with another person, make sure to post to our #devhacks channel or create an issue under the DevHack repo.

Stay connected

Keep in touch on Slack by joining our #devhacks channel.
